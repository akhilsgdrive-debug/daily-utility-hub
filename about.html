Built for people who just want things to work.

Daily Utility Hub was created because we were tired of bloated websites, sign-up walls, and tools that track you just to do simple math or keep a to-do list.

Every tool here runs entirely in your browser. Your notes, todos, calculations, and generated passwords never leave your device. There is no backend, no database, and no analytics.

Our principles

  • 01 Privacy first — localStorage only
  • 02 Fast & lightweight — no heavy frameworks
  • 03 Beautiful by default — clean modern interface
  • 04 Works offline after first load
  • 05 Completely free, forever

The entire project is open source and designed to be easy to fork, modify, and host yourself on GitHub Pages or any static host.
