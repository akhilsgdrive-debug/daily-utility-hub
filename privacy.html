Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

Short version: We don't collect any personal data. Everything happens locally in your browser.

What data do we collect?

Nothing. Daily Utility Hub is a completely static website with client-side JavaScript. We do not use cookies for tracking, we do not have a backend, and we do not log IP addresses or any user behavior.

Local Storage

Some tools (Notes and To-Do List) use your browser's localStorage to save your data between visits. This data stays on your device and is never sent to any server. You can clear it anytime from your browser settings.

Third-party services

We use Tailwind CSS via CDN for styling. No other third-party trackers or analytics are loaded.

Your rights

Since we store no data about you, there is nothing to request, delete, or export from our side. All control is in your hands via the tools themselves and your browser.

This site is open source. Feel free to self-host it yourself.
