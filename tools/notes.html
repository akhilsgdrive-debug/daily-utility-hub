Productivity Tips for Better Note Taking

  1. 1. Use clear, descriptive titles — "Q3 Marketing Ideas" is much better than "Ideas".
  2. 2. Star your most important notes — Keep critical information easily accessible.
  3. 3. Write in short paragraphs — Improves readability when reviewing later.
  4. 4. Use Markdown in preview — Add headings, lists, and emphasis for structured notes.
  5. 5. Export regularly — Create personal backups of your knowledge base.
  6. 6. Review starred notes weekly — Turn captured ideas into real action.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is this notes app really free?

Yes. Completely free with no hidden costs, premium tiers, or advertisements. We built it as a genuinely useful tool for everyone.

Where are my notes stored?

Your notes are stored securely in your browser's local storage. They never leave your device and are not sent to any server.

Can I access my notes on another device?

Currently, notes are device-specific for maximum privacy. You can easily export your notes as JSON and import them on any other device or browser.

Does it support Markdown?

Yes! Write normally and switch to the Preview tab to see beautifully rendered Markdown including headings, lists, bold, italic, and more.

What happens if I clear my browser data?

Your notes will be lost. We strongly recommend exporting important notes regularly as a backup.

Is there a limit to how many notes I can create?

No limits. Create as many notes as you want. The app is designed to feel fast even with hundreds of notes.
